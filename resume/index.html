Career Profile

I'm all about Java and I'm passionate about learning everything I can that will further my clients in order to provide the best possible value for their business. While technology is important, adhering to practices that have been proven to work are equally is not more important. Agile methologies, domain driven design, SOLID principles, clean code and architecture, code reviews and test-driven development are only a few of the key pillars I find indispensable to write good software.

That being said, first and foremost I am a software craftsman. I adhere to professional principles that have proven to be the most succesful for all parties. While my expertise lies with Java and JVM related technologies, what programming language is being used isn't really that important to me.

Frameworks and technologies are a means to an end. The goal of using any technology should be to better deliver business value and if (or when) I can prove a certain technology enables me to deliver the needs of the business more quickly, I'll be championing to adopt that technology.

I love working together with other people, learning and mentoring at the same time. Sharing my opinion with passion is something I love and and I'm not afraid to say what I think, even if it's in front of a crowd. Speaking at conferences at every chance I get is only an extension of that belief.
